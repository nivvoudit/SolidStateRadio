ABOUT US


We are Solid State Radio, a dynamic Rock and Roll band merging

the soulful roots of Black American music with the infectious

energy of Britpop.


With a fusion that transcends borders, our sound is a captivating

journey through rhythm and melody, crafting a unique sonic

experience that resonates with diverse musical influences.


Get ready to groove to the soulful beats and Britpop vibes of
